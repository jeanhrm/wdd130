Guide in your adventure

At Willka Rafting Adventures, our mission is to share the thrill of river rafting with people from all walks of life. We are passionate about respect for nature, local culture, and adventure!

History

Founded in 2021 by local river lovers, our company began with the goal of promoting sustainable tourism in the Peruvian Andes. What started as weekend trips on the Ichu River has grown into multiple river adventures, guided by experienced professionals.
